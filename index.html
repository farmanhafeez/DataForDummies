Why DataForDummies?

There are plenty of great data mocking libraries available for almost every language and platform. But not everyone is a programmer or has time to learn a new framework. Data For Dummies allows you to quickly and easily to download large amounts of randomly generated test data based on your own specs which you can then load directly into your test environment. Check our Documentation for the details and procedure to use the data.
